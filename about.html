Our
Story.

MalPal started in 2023, by a group of three senior bioinformatics students. They idea sprang up upon realizing the significant issue of malnutrition in hospitals. They came to the realization that artificial intelligence could be used to help solve this issue. And so, they began building a pal to help deal with mal-nutrition: MalPal.
