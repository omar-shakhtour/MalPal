MalPal.

Revolutionizing malnutrition detection using AI.

MalPal is a aritifical intelligence-powered software that detects malnutrition in hospital patients. Using AI image detection, MalPal analzyes a patients food intake and calculates nutritional intake using it's comphrensive database of nutritional foods.

How it
works.

MalPal uses a 275 megapixel camera installed in hospitals to take a snapshot of a patient's food tray. Using the MalPal AI software, MalPal analyzes the contents of the snapshot and uploads the nutritional content of the food on the tray. A patient who is not intaking sufficient levels of nutritional nourishment, will be easily identified by the MalPal system. MalPal will then alert healthcare professionals about potential malnutrition.
